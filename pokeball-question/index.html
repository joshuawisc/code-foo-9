According to Bulbapedia the size of a Pokeball switches between that of a baseball or a ping pong ball by pressing a button. My calculations will be under the assumption that the pokeballs are full sized.

The Pokeballs are packed in a HCP lattice to produce highest average density of 74% of total volume (From Wikipedia)

74% of Volume of Tower = 0.74 * 6082.12
= 4500.77 m3

Amount of Pokeballs =
Total volume occupied by balls / Volume per ball
= 4500.77 / 2.21 * 10^-4 m3
= 20,365,475 Pokeballs

ANSWER = 20,365,475 Pokeballs
